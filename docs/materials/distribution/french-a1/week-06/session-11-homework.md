## Part 1 — Partitive Articles: Fill in the Form

Complete each sentence with *du, de la, de l',* or *des.* No answers provided — check using the rule (masculine singular / feminine singular / vowel or h muet / plural).

1. Je voudrais _____ café, s'il vous plaît.
2. Elle prend _____ eau gazeuse.
3. Il mange _____ croissants le matin.
4. Nous prenons _____ bière et _____ fromage.
5. Tu veux _____ jus d'orange ou _____ thé?
6. Je prends _____ tarte au citron.
7. Ils mangent _____ pain avec _____ beurre.
8. Vous prenez _____ salade?

**Check using your Week 6 Vocabulary Reference:** look up the gender of each noun (m. or f.) if you are unsure.

---

## Part 2 — Negation: Change to Negative (5 sentences)

Rewrite each sentence in the negative. Remember: partitive → *de* (or *d'*) after *ne...pas.*

1. Je prends du café.
2. Elle mange de la tarte.
3. Il boit de l'eau.
4. Nous prenons des croissants.
5. Je mange du fromage le soir.

**Answer key (check your work):**
1. Je ne prends **pas de** café.
2. Elle ne mange **pas de** tarte.
3. Il ne boit **pas d'**eau.
4. Nous ne prenons **pas de** croissants.
5. Je ne mange **pas de** fromage le soir.

---

## Part 3 — Write Your Café Order (5 sentences minimum)

Imagine you are at a French café. Write what you would order — using *je voudrais* or *je vais prendre.* Include:

- [ ] At least 2 drinks (one using a partitive article, one using *un/une*)
- [ ] At least 1 food item
- [ ] At least 1 negative sentence (*Je ne prends pas de...*)
- [ ] Your order ending: *C'est tout, merci.*

**Example format:**
> *Bonjour. Je voudrais un café et de la tarte aux pommes, s'il vous plaît. Je ne prends pas de bière. Et une eau plate aussi. C'est tout, merci.*

Write your own — do not copy the example.

---

## Part 4 — Speaking Practice

Read your café order from Part 3 out loud three times. Third try: no notes. You should be able to say it smoothly — this is the kind of thing you might need to say in a real café within weeks of completing this level.

---

## Extension (Optional)

Look at a real French café menu online. Find:
1. One item you recognize from class.
2. One item you do not know — look it up and write a sentence using it: *Je voudrais + [item].*
3. One item with a partitive article in its description (e.g., *avec du fromage, avec de la crème*).

You do not need to share this — it is for your own curiosity.
