# Spanish A1 — Session 7 Homework

**Complete before Session 8.**
**Estimated time: 20–25 minutes**

---

## Part 1 — Verb Endings from Memory (5 min)

Fill in the endings from memory, then check:

| Subject | -ar | -er | -ir |
|---|---|---|---|
| yo | ___ | ___ | ___ |
| tú | ___ | ___ | ___ |
| él/ella | ___ | ___ | ___ |
| nosotros | ___ | ___ | ___ |
| vosotros | ___ | ___ | ___ |
| ellos | ___ | ___ | ___ |

**Answers:**

| Subject | -ar | -er | -ir |
|---|---|---|---|
| yo | -o | -o | -o |
| tú | -as | -es | -es |
| él/ella | -a | -e | -e |
| nosotros | -amos | -emos | -imos |
| vosotros | -áis | -éis | -ís |
| ellos | -an | -en | -en |

Then say the full conjugation of *comer* and *vivir* out loud without looking.

---

## Part 2 — Written Sentences (10 min)

Write 6 sentences about yourself — at least one from each verb family.

**Your sentences must include:**
1. One -ar verb sentence (any verb from Week 3)
2. One -ar verb negative
3. Two -er verb sentences (use 2 different verbs: *comer, beber, leer, comprender*)
4. Two -ir verb sentences (use *vivir* and one other: *escribir, recibir*)

**Example:**
> *Trabajo de nueve a seis. No descanso mucho los lunes. Como en la oficina de lunes a miércoles. Bebo agua durante el día — no bebo café por la tarde. Vivo en el centro de la ciudad. Escribo correos todo el día.*

---

## Part 3 — Speaking Practice (5 min)

Say your 6 sentences out loud twice. On the second time, no notes.

Then describe a person you know in 3 sentences — one verb per family — using *él/ella* forms:
> *Mi jefe vive cerca de la oficina. Come fuera todos los días. Trabaja demasiado.*

---

## Vocabulary Quick-Check

Give the correct conjugated form:

1. *yo + leer* → _______________
2. *ella + vivir* → _______________
3. *ellos + comer* → _______________
4. *tú + beber* → _______________
5. *él + trabajar* → _______________

**Answers:** 1. leo · 2. vive · 3. comen · 4. bebes · 5. trabaja

---

## Optional Extension

**All six forms.** Write the full conjugation of *escribir* and *hablar* from memory. Then write one sentence for each of the six subject pronouns for each verb.

Or: look up one new verb you want to be able to say — identify its family (-ar, -er, or -ir) and write it in the *yo* and *él/ella* forms.
