# Spanish A1 — Session 8 Homework

**Complete before Session 9 (first session of Week 5).**
**Estimated time: 20–25 minutes**

---

## Part 1 — Reflexive Pronoun Table from Memory (3 min)

Cover the table and fill it in, then check:

| Subject | Reflexive pronoun |
|---|---|
| yo | ___ |
| tú | ___ |
| él / ella / usted | ___ |
| nosotros/as | ___ |
| vosotros/as | ___ |
| ellos / ellas / ustedes | ___ |

**Answers:** yo → me · tú → te · él/ella → se · nosotros → nos · vosotros → os · ellos → se

Then say *me levanto, te levantas, se levanta, nos levantamos* out loud without looking.

---

## Part 2 — Complete Routine Paragraph (12 min)

Write your complete daily routine in 5–7 sentences. This is the Week 4 writing milestone.

**Your paragraph must include:**
1. At least 3 different reflexive verbs
2. At least 2 regular -ar, -er, or -ir verbs from Weeks 3–4
3. At least 2 specific clock times or approximate times with *hacia las*
4. At least 2 sequencing words (*primero, luego, después, finalmente*)
5. At least one negative sentence

**Example:**
> *Me despierto a las seis y media, pero me levanto a las siete. Primero me ducho y luego me preparo — tardo unos veinte minutos. Después como algo rápido y bebo un café. Trabajo de nueve a seis. Por la noche, leo un poco o miro algo en la tele. Me acuesto hacia las once. No me acuesto nunca antes de las diez.*

Write yours in your notebook or document.

---

## Part 3 — Time Expression Practice (4 min)

Write these times in Spanish:

1. 7:00 a.m. → _______________
2. 7:30 a.m. → _______________
3. 1:00 p.m. (mediodía) → _______________
4. 6:00 p.m. → _______________
5. 11:00 p.m. → _______________

**Answers:**
1. *Son las siete (de la mañana).*
2. *Son las siete y media.*
3. *Es mediodía.* / *Es la una.*
4. *Son las seis (de la tarde).*
5. *Son las once (de la noche).*

---

## Part 4 — Speaking (5 min)

Say your complete routine paragraph out loud twice. On the second time, no notes.

Then answer these questions out loud without preparation:
- *¿A qué hora te levantas normalmente?*
- *¿Te duchas por la mañana o por la noche?*
- *¿A qué hora te acuestas?*

---

## Optional Extension

**Describe someone else's routine.** Write 3–4 sentences about a partner, family member, or colleague — morning through evening. Use *él/ella se...* for reflexive verbs and *él/ella + verb* for other actions. Include at least two clock times.

Try one negative reflexive sentence: *No se levanta nunca antes de las ocho.* Remember: *no* goes before the reflexive pronoun.
