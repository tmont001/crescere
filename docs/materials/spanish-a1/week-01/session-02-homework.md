## Part 1 — Numbers 31–100: The Patterns (10 min)

You learned 1–20 in Session 1 and 21–30 in Session 1 homework. Here is the rest of the system. Unlike French, Spanish 31–99 are completely regular.

### 31–99: tens + y + unit (three separate words)

| Tens | | | |
|---|---|---|---|
| 30 — treinta | 40 — cuarenta | 50 — cincuenta | 60 — sesenta |
| 70 — setenta | 80 — ochenta | 90 — noventa | |

**Pattern:** tens + *y* + unit from 1–9

Examples: 35 = *treinta y cinco* · 42 = *cuarenta y dos* · 57 = *cincuenta y siete* · 63 = *sesenta y tres* · 71 = *setenta y uno* · 88 = *ochenta y ocho* · 99 = *noventa y nueve*

**Note:** Unlike 21–29 (written as one word: *veintiuno, veintidós...*), the numbers 31+ are always three separate words.

### 100

100 = *cien* (when alone) / *ciento* (when followed by more: *ciento uno, ciento cincuenta*)

**Practice:** Write these numbers in Spanish words. Do not look back.

1. 47 → _______________
2. 83 → _______________
3. 62 → _______________
4. 71 → _______________
5. 99 → _______________
6. 100 → _______________

**Answers:** 1. cuarenta y siete · 2. ochenta y tres · 3. sesenta y dos · 4. setenta y uno · 5. noventa y nueve · 6. cien

## Part 2 — Written Introduction (8 min)

Write five sentences in Spanish introducing yourself. Use *ser* and the phrases from Sessions 1 and 2.

Your sentences must include:
1. Your name (*Me llamo...*)
2. Where you are from (*Soy de...*)
3. Your nationality (*Soy...*)
4. Your profession (*Soy...*)
5. One more sentence using *ser* — for example, a form other than *soy* (describe someone else, or say what the group is).

**Example:**
> *Me llamo Marcos. Soy de Buenos Aires. Soy argentino. Soy ingeniero. Nosotros somos estudiantes de español.*

Write yours in your notebook or document.

---

## Part 3 — Speaking Practice (5 min)

Say your written introduction out loud three times. On the third try, close your notes and say it from memory. It should take under 30 seconds.

Then answer these questions out loud, as if someone is asking you:

- *¿Cómo te llamas?* → *Me llamo...*
- *¿De dónde eres?* → *Soy de...*
- *¿A qué te dedicas?* → *Soy...*
