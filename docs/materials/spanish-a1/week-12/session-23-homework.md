# Spanish A1 — Session 23 Homework
## Final Performance Preparation

**Estimated time:** 25–30 minutes
**Due:** Before Session 24 — this is the most important homework of the course

---

## Overview

Session 24 is your final performance. This homework prepares you for it. Everything here is directly useful for tomorrow.

---

## Part 1 — Role Play Preparation (10 min out loud)

Choose one of the three scenario options from the final performance guide:

- **Option A:** You meet someone at a café, market, or language event. You get talking; you make a plan.
- **Option B:** You are helping a Spanish-speaking visitor navigate your city.
- **Option C:** You meet at a language exchange. You both describe yourselves and your lives, and make a plan.

Once you have chosen, practice the four topic areas out loud — not in your head.

**Topic area 1 — Introduction with ser and estar (2 min)**
Say aloud: your name, where you are from, what you do (ser). Then add how you feel right now and something about your current situation (estar). Then add one personal detail. Say this three times.

*Key check: Did you use ser for identity/origin? Did you use estar for a state or location?*

**Topic area 2 — Making a plan (2 min)**
Say aloud: propose an activity using *ir a + infinitivo*, suggest a day and time, ask if the other person is free, agree on a meeting point. Say this twice.

**Topic area 3 — Directions (2 min)**
Choose a real or invented place in your city. Give directions from a café or supermarket to that place — at least 4 direction steps. Use: *todo recto, a la izquierda, a la derecha, al lado de, enfrente de.* Say this twice.

**Topic area 4 — Describe a place (2 min)**
Describe your home or neighbourhood in 3–4 sentences. Include: *hay..., está(n)..., me gusta...* Say this twice.

**Your repair phrases — know these cold:**
- *¿Puede repetir, por favor?*
- *¿Cómo se dice [word] en español?*
- *Estoy buscando la palabra para...*

## Part 2 — Written Message Draft (12 min)

Write your Part B written message. This should be 8–10 sentences.

**Checklist — before you write:**
- [ ] I know what *ser* sentences I will use (identity, origin, profession)
- [ ] I know what *estar* sentence I will use (state, emotion, or location)
- [ ] I know 2 sentences about my daily routine (with reflexive verbs if possible)
- [ ] I know something I enjoy (*me gusta / me encanta + gustar agreement*)
- [ ] I know how to suggest meeting (*ir a + infinitivo* or *quisiera*)
- [ ] I know one question to ask the other person

**After writing, check:**
- [ ] Ser vs. estar: did you use the right one in each sentence?
- [ ] *Gustar* agreement: *me gusta* (singular/infinitive) or *me gustan* (plural)?
- [ ] Article contractions: *al* not *a el*; *del* not *de el*
- [ ] *Ir a + infinitivo*: is the *a* there? (*voy a quedar*, not *voy quedar*)
- [ ] At least one question at the end

---

## Part 3 — Quick Grammar Check (5 min)

Write these from memory — no notes:

1. The full conjugation of *ir* (6 forms)
2. The full conjugation of *estar* (6 forms)
3. The four ser/estar rule triggers — one example each: identity (ser), state (estar), location of person/thing (estar), permanent characteristic (ser)

**Answer key:**
1. *voy / vas / va / vamos / vais / van*
2. *estoy / estás / está / estamos / estáis / están*
3. *Soy médico* (identity) / *Estoy cansado* (state) / *El banco está enfrente* (location) / *Es inteligente* (characteristic)

If you missed the estar conjugation: *estoy, estás, está, estamos, estáis, están* — write it five times. This verb is in the final performance.

**Written message:**

## Extension (Optional)

If you want to go further:
- Record yourself doing the role play practice out loud. Listen back. Do the *ser/estar* choices feel natural? Is *ir a + infinitivo* coming out smoothly?
- Write a second scenario: if you chose Option A, practice Option B briefly — makes you more flexible in Session 24.
- Try writing 3 sentences that would require *ser* and 3 that would require *estar* in a new context (your family, a place you visited). The more contexts you use it in, the more natural it becomes.
