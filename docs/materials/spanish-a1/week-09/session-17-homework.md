# Spanish A1 — Session 17 Homework
## Near Future: Ir a + Infinitivo

**Estimated time:** 20–30 minutes
**Due:** Before Session 18

---

## Part 1 — Ir a + Infinitivo: Transformation (Written, 5 min)

Rewrite each sentence in the near future using *ir a + infinitivo.*

1. *Trabajo mañana.* →
2. *Llama a su madre esta noche.* →
3. *Viajamos la semana que viene.* →
4. *Cocinas esta tarde.* →
5. *Salen en dos días.* →
6. *No duermo esta noche.* →

**Answer key:**
1. *Voy a trabajar mañana.*
2. *Va a llamar a su madre esta noche.*
3. *Vamos a viajar la semana que viene.*
4. *Vas a cocinar esta tarde.*
5. *Van a salir en dos días.*
6. *No voy a dormir esta noche.*

---

## Part 2 — Future Time Expressions (Written, 5 min)

Fill in the blank with the most natural time expression. More than one answer may be correct.

1. *Voy a ir de vacaciones ___________.* (next month)
2. *Va a llamar ___________.* (tomorrow morning)
3. *Vamos a cenar juntos ___________.* (tonight)
4. *Va a volver ___________.* (in two days)
5. *¿Vas a viajar a España ___________?* (next year)
6. *Vamos a quedar ___________.* (this weekend)

**Answer key:**
1. *el mes que viene*
2. *mañana por la mañana*
3. *esta noche*
4. *en dos días*
5. *el año que viene*
6. *este fin de semana*

---

## Part 3 — Written Week Plan (12 min)

Write a plan for your coming week. Use 6–8 sentences. Include:

- [ ] At least 4 *voy a + infinitivo* sentences with different verbs
- [ ] At least 3 different time expressions
- [ ] At least 1 negative plan (*no voy a...*)
- [ ] At least 1 plan involving another person (*vamos a / va a*)

**Example structure (use as a guide, not a template):**
> *Esta tarde voy a estudiar y preparar la cena. Mañana voy a trabajar todo el día. No voy a salir mañana por la noche — tengo que acostarme pronto. Este fin de semana vamos a ver a unos amigos. La semana que viene pensamos ir a la playa si hace buen tiempo.*

---

## Part 5 — Speaking Practice (5 min out loud)

Read your week plan aloud THREE times.
- First time: read from your notes
- Second time: glance at notes only for vocabulary
- Third time: say it without notes

**On the third try, aim for 4 sentences without reading.** This is the level you'll need for the Week 9 role play in Session 18.

## Extension (Optional)

If you finish early:
- Write a short message to an imaginary Spanish-speaking friend: propose one activity for the coming week. Include a time and a place. Use *ir a + infinitivo.*
- Write 2 sentences using *pensar + infinitivo* for uncertain future plans: *Pienso ir a...* / *Pensamos visitar...*
- Write 2 sentences about a long-term dream plan: *Algún día voy a...*
