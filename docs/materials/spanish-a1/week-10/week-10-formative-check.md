# Spanish A1 — Week 10 Formative Check
## Hobbies and Leisure

**When to administer:** At the start of Session 21, or as a closing check at the end of Session 20.
**Format:** Oral section first (5 prompts); written section second (5 prompts).
**Purpose:** Diagnostic tool. Not a grade. Informs whether the cohort is ready to progress to Week 11 content (*doler, body parts, pharmacy scenario*) or needs reinforcement of Week 10 structures.

---

## Oral Prompts (5 prompts — instructor reads aloud)

Administer to each student individually or in round-robin. Allow 10–15 seconds per prompt.

**Prompt 1:** "Tell me two sports or activities you do. Use the correct verb for each."
*Target:* One *jugar a* sentence (with correct *al/a la/a las* contraction) + one *hacer + activity* sentence (no article).
*Minimum pass:* Both structures correct. "Juego de fútbol" → fail. "Hago el yoga" → fail.

**Prompt 2:** "Tell me one activity you never or rarely do, and why."
*Target:* *Nunca juego al...* or *Casi nunca hago...* + a brief reason.
*Watch for:* *Nunca + verb* (correct, no *no* needed); vs. *No + verb + nunca* (also correct). Accept both.

**Prompt 3:** "Give me the *yo* and the *ellos* forms of *preferir.* Use *prefiero* in a sentence."
*Target:* *prefiero / prefieren.* Sentence: *Prefiero el fútbol al baloncesto.*
*Watch for:* *yo prefero* (→ *prefiero*, e→ie); *ellos preferen* (→ *prefieren*).

**Prompt 4:** "Tell me one thing you love and one thing you hate in your free time."
*Target:* *Me encanta + noun/infinitive* AND *Odio + article + noun.*
*Watch for:* *Me encanto el tenis* (→ *me **encanta** el tenis*); *odio de los videojuegos* (→ *odio **los** videojuegos*, no *de*).

**Prompt 5:** "Tell me a preference — what do you prefer, and compared to what?"
*Target:* *Prefiero A al B.* Or *Me gusta más A que B.*
*Minimum pass:* A comparison using any of the structures from Session 20, correctly formed with articles.

---

## Written Prompts (5 items — students write independently)

**Item 1:** Write one *jugar a* sentence and one *hacer* sentence about your own hobbies.
*Expected:* *Juego al fútbol.* / *Hago yoga.* (or other sports/activities)
*Watch for:* *Juego de fútbol* (missing *al*); *Hago el yoga* (article after *hacer* — should have none).

**Item 2:** Rewrite in the negative: *Juego al golf todos los días.*
*Expected:* *No juego al golf todos los días.* (or *Nunca juego al golf.* if not specifying *todos los días*)
*Watch for:* *No juego de golf* — the *al* stays; only *no* is added.

**Item 3:** Choose *gustar, encantar,* or *odiar* and write a sentence about a leisure activity. Use the correct form.
*Expected:* *Me encanta el cine.* / *Odio el golf.* / *Me gustan los deportes.*
*Watch for:* Wrong *gustar/encantar* form (me gustan el cine — singular should be *me gusta*).

**Item 4:** Write the *yo* form of *preferir.* Use it in a sentence comparing two activities or places.
*Expected:* *prefiero.* Sentence: *Prefiero el fútbol al baloncesto.* / *Prefiero la montaña al mar.*
*Watch for:* *yo prefero* (→ *prefiero*); missing *al* in the comparison (*prefiero el fútbol el baloncesto* — needs *al*).

**Item 5:** Write 3–4 sentences about your leisure life that include: at least one *jugar a* or *hacer*, a frequency adverb, and an opinion using *gustar / encantar / preferir.*
*Expected:* Connected sentences that demonstrate natural integration of all three.
*Minimum pass:* All three elements present; *al* contraction correct; *gustar/encantar* verb agreement correct.

---

## Scoring Decision Table

| Student output | Decision |
|---|---|
| All 5 oral prompts pass + 4–5 written correct | Ready to move on to Week 11 |
| 4 oral pass + 3–4 written correct | Ready to move on — monitor *preferir* stem change in Week 11 speaking |
| *Jugar a / hacer* correct but *gustar* forms still shaky | Move to Week 11 — give one *gustar* drill at Session 21 open (connects to *doler*) |
| *Preferir* stem change not yet consistent | Move to Week 11 — assign *preferir* as conjugation review homework |
| *Encantar* form (*me encanta vs. me encanto*) still wrong | Move to Week 11 — address in Session 21 warm-up; same structure as *doler* |
| Fewer than 3 oral prompts pass OR fewer than 3 written correct | Flag for instructor follow-up; do not delay Week 11 for whole cohort |

---

## Ready to Move On

Before beginning Week 11 (*doler*, body parts, pharmacy), a student should be able to:
- [ ] Produce *juego al* correctly for 3+ sports (with *al* contraction)
- [ ] Produce *hago + activity* without an article for 3+ activities
- [ ] Use at least 3 frequency adverbs fluently
- [ ] Conjugate *preferir* correctly in *yo* form (*prefiero*)
- [ ] Express a preference comparing two things (*prefiero A al B*)
- [ ] Use *encantar* with correct agreement (*encanta* for singular/infinitive, *encantan* for plural)
- [ ] Use *odiar* as a regular -ar verb with the definite article

---

## Not a Gate

The following items are NOT required for progression to Week 11:
- *Tanto como* comparison (extension material)
- Full *preferir* table mastered in all 6 forms (only *yo* and *ellos* required at this stage)
- *Practicar* as a distinct verb from *hacer* (introduced for awareness only)
- Extended cultural activities vocabulary beyond 5–6 items
- Regional sport vocabulary variation (béisbol, pádel) beyond one mention
- Written output beyond 4–6 coherent sentences on the topic
