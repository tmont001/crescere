# Spanish A1 — Session 5 Homework

**Complete before Session 6.**
**Estimated time: 20–25 minutes**

---

## Part 1 — The -ar Ending from Memory (5 min)

Cover the table below and say all six endings from memory. Then apply them to *hablar* and to one more verb of your choice.

| Subject | Ending | Hablar |
|---|---|---|
| yo | -o | hablo |
| tú | -as | hablas |
| él / ella / usted | -a | habla |
| nosotros/as | -amos | hablamos |
| vosotros/as | -áis | habláis |
| ellos / ellas / ustedes | -an | hablan |

Say *hablo, hablas, habla, hablamos, habláis, hablan* three times out loud without looking.

Then write out the full conjugation of *trabajar* from memory.

---

## Part 2 — Written Sentences (10 min)

Write 5 sentences about yourself in Spanish. Use 5 different -ar verbs. At least one sentence must be negative.

**Your sentences must include:**
1. Where you work or what you study (*trabajar* or *estudiar*)
2. Something you listen to (*escuchar*)
3. Something you watch (*mirar*)
4. Something you do or do not do on weekends (*trabajar, descansar, comprar*...)
5. Something about your morning or evening routine (*preparar, tomar*...)

**Example:**
> *Trabajo en un colegio. Estudio italiano los martes. Escucho música clásica por la mañana. Miro pocas series — no tengo mucho tiempo. Los fines de semana, descanso y preparo la comida.*

Write yours in your notebook or document.

---

## Part 3 — Speaking Practice (5 min)

Say your 5 sentences out loud three times. On the third time, do it without looking at your notes.

Then describe a friend or family member in 2–3 sentences using *él/ella* forms:
> *Mi hermano se llama Carlos. Trabaja en un banco. No estudia ahora — trabaja mucho.*

---

## Vocabulary Quick-Check

Translate without looking at your notes:

1. She works in a hospital → _______________
2. We listen to music → _______________
3. I don't watch TV → _______________
4. They (masc.) study English → _______________
5. Do you buy food at the market? → _______________

**Answers:**
1. *Ella trabaja en un hospital.*
2. *Escuchamos música.*
3. *No miro la tele.*
4. *Estudian inglés.*
5. *¿Compras comida en el mercado?*

## Optional Extension

**Practice all six forms.** Choose one more verb from the week's list and write one sentence for each of the six subject forms. It does not have to make perfect sense — the goal is the pattern.

Try: *necesitar* (to need) — *Necesito, necesitas, necesita, necesitamos, necesitáis, necesitan.*
