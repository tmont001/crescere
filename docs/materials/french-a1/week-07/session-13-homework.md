## Part 1 — Demonstratives: Ce, Cet, Cette, Ces

Supply the correct demonstrative adjective for each noun. No answer key — use the rules (m. before consonant / m. before vowel or h muet / f. / plural).

1. _____ manteau (m., consonant)

## Part 2 — Color Agreement (5 sentences)

Write each phrase with the color correctly agreeing with the clothing noun. Check the vocabulary file for genders if needed.

1. une chemise + blanc
2. un pantalon + gris
3. des chaussures + marron *(invariable!)*
4. une robe + bleu
5. des baskets + blanc

**Answer key:**
1. une chemise **blanche**
2. un pantalon **gris**
3. des chaussures **marron** *(invariable — never changes)*
4. une robe **bleue**
5. des baskets **blanches**

---

## Part 3 — Shopping Dialogue (6 lines minimum)

Write a short dialogue between a customer (*C*) and a shopkeeper (*V* for vendeur/vendeuse). You are the customer.

**Required elements:**
- [ ] Customer states what they're looking for: *Je cherche...*
- [ ] Customer uses a demonstrative: *ce / cet / cette / ces + [item]*
- [ ] Customer asks about a color or size: *Vous avez... en [color], taille [M/L]?*
- [ ] Customer asks to try it on: *Je peux l'essayer?*
- [ ] Customer asks the price: *C'est combien?*
- [ ] Customer buys or declines

---

## Part 4 — Speaking Practice

Read your dialogue from Part 3 out loud. Say both parts — customer and shopkeeper. Third try: no notes on the core shopping phrases (*Je cherche..., Je peux l'essayer?, C'est combien?*).

Then try describing what you are wearing right now in French. Use at least 3 clothing items with colors: *Je porte un jean bleu et un tee-shirt blanc...*

---

## Extension (Optional)

Write 3 sentences describing what someone you know is wearing. Use *il porte / elle porte* and include color agreement for each item.
