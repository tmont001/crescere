# French A1 — Session 17 Homework
## Near Future: Aller + Infinitif

**Estimated time:** 20–30 minutes
**Due:** Before Session 18

---

## Part 1 — Aller + Infinitif: Transformation (Written, 5 min)

Rewrite each sentence in the near future using *aller + infinitif.*

1. *Je travaille demain.* →
2. *Elle appelle sa mère ce soir.* →
3. *Nous voyageons la semaine prochaine.* →
4. *Tu fais la cuisine ce soir.* →
5. *Ils partent dans deux jours.* →
6. *Je ne dors pas ce soir.* →

**Answer key:**
1. *Je vais travailler demain.*
2. *Elle va appeler sa mère ce soir.*
3. *Nous allons voyager la semaine prochaine.*
4. *Tu vas faire la cuisine ce soir.*
5. *Ils vont partir dans deux jours.*
6. *Je ne vais pas dormir ce soir.*

---

## Part 2 — Future Time Expressions (Written, 5 min)

Fill in the blank with the most natural time expression. More than one answer may be correct.

1. *Je vais partir en vacances ___________.* (next month)
2. *Elle va appeler ___________.* (tomorrow morning)
3. *Nous allons dîner ensemble ___________.* (this evening)
4. *Il va rentrer ___________.* (in two days)
5. *Tu vas voyager à Paris ___________?* (next year)
6. *On va se retrouver ___________.* (this weekend)

**Answer key:**
1. *le mois prochain*
2. *demain matin*
3. *ce soir*
4. *dans deux jours*
5. *l'année prochaine*
6. *ce week-end*

---

## Part 3 — Written Week Plan (15 min)

Write a plan for your coming week. Use 6–8 sentences. Include:

- [ ] At least 4 *je vais + infinitif* sentences with different verbs
- [ ] At least 3 different time expressions
- [ ] At least 1 negative plan (*je ne vais pas...*)
- [ ] At least 1 plan involving another person (*nous allons / on va / il/elle va*)

**Example structure (use as a guide, not a template):**
> *Ce soir, je vais faire la cuisine et regarder un film. Demain, je vais travailler toute la journée. Je ne vais pas sortir demain soir — je dois me coucher tôt. Ce week-end, nous allons voir des amis. La semaine prochaine, on va partir en week-end à la campagne.*

## Part 5 — Speaking Practice (5 min out loud)

Read your week plan aloud THREE times.
- First time: read from your notes
- Second time: glance at notes only for vocabulary
- Third time: say it without notes

**On the third try, aim for 4 sentences without reading.** This is the level you'll need for the Week 9 role play in Session 18.

## Extension (Optional)

If you finish early:
