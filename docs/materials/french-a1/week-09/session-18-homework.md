## Part 1 — Devoir Conjugation from Memory (5 min)

Write the full conjugation of *devoir* WITHOUT looking at your notes. Check below when done.

| je | tu | il/elle | nous | vous | ils/elles |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| | | | | | |

**Answer key:**

| je | tu | il/elle | nous | vous | ils/elles |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| dois | dois | doit | devons | devez | doivent |

If you missed *vous devez* or *ils doivent*, repeat those forms 5 times.

## Part 2 — Devoir, Vouloir, or Pouvoir? (Written, 8 min)

Fill in the blank with the correct form of *devoir, vouloir,* or *pouvoir.* Each verb is used at least once.

1. *Je ___________ aller au cinéma ce soir, mais je ___________ travailler.* (I want... I have to)
2. *Tu ___________ venir à 18h?* (Can you)
3. *Elle ___________ appeler son médecin — elle est malade depuis trois jours.* (needs to)
4. *Nous ___________ voyager l'année prochaine, mais nous ne ___________ pas — c'est trop cher.* (want to... can't)
5. *Vous ___________ réserver à l'avance pour ce restaurant.* (have to / must)
6. *Ils ___________ partir maintenant — leur train est à 20h.* (have to)
7. *Je ___________ vous aider si vous ___________.* (can... want)

**Answer key:**
1. *veux / dois*
2. *Peux*
3. *doit*
4. *voulons / pouvons*
5. *devez*
6. *doivent*
7. *peux / voulez*

## Part 3 — Written Planning Note (15 min)

The curriculum writing task for Sessions 17–18: write a short note to a French contact about upcoming plans. Use 5–7 sentences.

Include all of the following at least once:
- [ ] *aller + infinitif* for a future plan
- [ ] *devoir + infinitif* for something you have to do
- [ ] *vouloir + infinitif* or *pouvoir + infinitif*
- [ ] At least 2 future time expressions
- [ ] A proposed activity together and a time/place

**Example structure (use as a guide, not a template):**
> *Salut Marie! Ce week-end, je vais rendre visite à mes parents en province. Mais la semaine prochaine, je suis libre. Je veux aller voir l'exposition au Grand Palais — tu veux venir? Je dois travailler lundi, mais mardi après-midi, je peux. On se retrouve devant le musée à 15h? Dis-moi si ça marche pour toi!*

## Part 4 — Speaking Practice (5 min out loud)

Read your planning note aloud THREE times.
- First time: read from your notes
- Second time: glance at notes only for vocabulary
- Third time: say it without notes

**On the third try, aim for the whole note from memory.** This is the level you'll need for the Week 9 formative check.

---

## Part 5 — Role Play Preparation (5 min)

The Week 9 formative check will include a planning exchange. Prepare by writing a 6-line script:

- Line 1 (A): Propose a plan. *Tu veux [activity] [time]?*
- Line 2 (B): Decline with a reason. *Je veux bien, mais je dois...*
- Line 3 (A): Suggest an alternative. *Et [alternative time], tu peux?*
- Line 4 (B): Accept or modify. *Oui, [alternative] ça marche! / Non, je dois aussi...*
- Line 5 (A): Confirm time/place. *On se retrouve à [time] devant [place]?*
- Line 6 (B): Confirm. *C'est parfait! À [time] alors!*
