# French A1 — Session 1 Homework

**Complete before Session 2.**
**Estimated time: 20–25 minutes**

---

## Part 1 — Say Your Introduction Out Loud (5 min)

Use the phrases from Session 1. Fill in your own information and say it out loud — not silently, not in writing. **Out loud.**

```
Bonjour. Je m'appelle _________.
Je suis de _________.
J'habite à _________.
```

Say it at least **three times in a row.** On the third time, close your notes and say it from memory.

This will feel uncomfortable at first. Do it anyway. That discomfort is the learning.

---

**Practice:** Count out loud from 1 to 30 twice — once looking, once without looking.

---

## Part 3 — Written Practice (5 min)

Write the following in French. Use what you learned in Session 1. Do not look anything up.

1. Write your greeting + your name: *Bonjour, je m'appelle...*
2. Write where you are from: *Je suis de...*
3. Write where you live: *J'habite à...*
4. Write the number 17 in French words.
5. Write the number 28 in French words.

## Optional Extension

If you finish the three parts and want to keep going:

- **Listen:** Find any 2-minute clip in French — a song, a trailer, a YouTube video. Just listen. Notice the r sound and the nasal vowels you heard in class. You do not need to understand the words.
- **Spell:** Practice spelling your first and last name using French letter names. Say each letter out loud.
