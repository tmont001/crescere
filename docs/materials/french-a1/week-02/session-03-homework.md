# French A1 — Session 3 Homework

**Complete before Session 4.**
**Estimated time: 20–25 minutes**

---

## Part 1 — Avoir Out Loud (5 min)

Cover the table below and say all six forms of *avoir* from memory. Then check.

| Subject | Avoir |
|---|---|
| j' | ai |
| tu | as |
| il / elle | a |
| nous | avons |
| vous | avez |
| ils / elles | ont |

Say the full conjugation three times out loud. On the third time, do it without looking.

Then say each of these out loud:
- *J'ai trente ans.*
- *Tu as les yeux bleus ?*
- *Elle a les cheveux bruns.*
- *Nous avons cours demain.*

---

## Part 2 — Written Description (10 min)

Write a 4–6 sentence description of yourself in French. Use vocabulary from Session 3.

**Your description must include:**
1. Your age (*J'ai... ans.*)
2. Your hair color and length (*J'ai les cheveux...*)
3. Your eye color (*J'ai les yeux...*)
4. Your height (*Je suis...*)
5. One optional additional detail — anything you know how to say

**Example:**
> *J'ai trente-deux ans. J'ai les cheveux châtains et courts. J'ai les yeux noisette. Je suis de taille moyenne. Je suis professeur.*

Write yours in your notebook or document.

---

## Part 3 — Speaking (5 min)

Say your written description out loud three times. On the third time, close your notes.

Then do the same for one other person — a family member, a well-known figure, or a classmate. Use *il/elle a...* and *il/elle est...* You do not need to write this one out — just say it.

---

## Vocabulary Quick-Check

Fill in the French without looking at your notes. Check against the vocabulary list.

1. long hair → les cheveux _______________
2. blue eyes → les yeux _______________
3. she is tall → elle est _______________
4. I have 45 years (age) → _______________
5. they have (ils) → _______________

**Answers:** 1. longs · 2. bleus · 3. grande · 4. J'ai quarante-cinq ans. · 5. ont

---

## Optional Extension

**Describe three more people.** Choose real people or well-known figures. For each, write 3 sentences using *avoir* and *être*. Aim to use at least three different colors and both *j'ai les cheveux...* and *j'ai les yeux...*

Try to include at least one person of each gender so you practice both *il a / elle est* and *il est / elle a* correctly.
