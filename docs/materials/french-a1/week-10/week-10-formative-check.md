# French A1 — Week 10 Formative Check
## Hobbies and Leisure

**When to administer:** At the start of Session 21, or as a closing check at the end of Session 20.
**Format:** Oral section first (5 prompts); written section second (5 prompts).
**Purpose:** Diagnostic tool. Not a grade. Informs whether the cohort is ready to progress to Week 11 content (*avoir mal à, body parts, pharmacy scenario*) or needs reinforcement of Week 10 structures.

---

## Oral Prompts (5 prompts — instructor reads aloud)

Administer to each student individually or in round-robin. Allow 10–15 seconds per prompt.

**Prompt 1:** "Tell me two sports or activities you do. Use the correct verb for each."
*Target:* One *jouer à* sentence (with correct *au/aux/à la* contraction) + one *faire de* sentence (with correct *du/de la/de l'* contraction).
*Minimum pass:* Both contractions correct. "Je joue du tennis" → fail on *jouer*. "Je fais au yoga" → fail on *faire.*

**Prompt 2:** "Tell me one activity you never or rarely do, and why."
*Target:* *Je ne joue jamais au / Je ne fais presque jamais de...* + a brief reason.
*Watch for:* *Je ne fais jamais du sport* (→ *je ne fais jamais **de** sport* — article collapses after negation).

**Prompt 3:** "Give me the *je* and the *ils* forms of *préférer.* Use *je préfère* in a sentence."
*Target:* *je préfère / ils préfèrent.* Sentence: *Je préfère la natation au golf.*
*Watch for:* *je préfere* (→ *préfère* with grave accent); *ils préferent* (→ *préfèrent*).

**Prompt 4:** "Tell me one thing you love and one thing you hate in your free time."
*Target:* *J'adore + le/la/les + activity* AND *Je déteste + le/la/les + activity.*
*Watch for:* *j'adore du cinéma* (→ *j'adore **le** cinéma* — definite article, not partitive); *je déteste du sport* (→ *je déteste **le** sport*).

**Prompt 5:** "Tell me a preference — what do you prefer, and compared to what?"
*Target:* *Je préfère A à/au B.* Or *J'aime A plus que B.*
*Minimum pass:* A comparison using any of the structures from Session 20, correctly formed.

---

## Written Prompts (5 items — students write independently)

**Item 1:** Write one *jouer à* sentence and one *faire de* sentence about your own hobbies.
*Expected:* *Je joue au tennis.* / *Je fais de la natation.* (or other sports/activities with correct contractions)
*Watch for:* Reversed prepositions (*joue du / fait au*).

**Item 2:** Rewrite in the negative: *Je fais du sport tous les jours.*
*Expected:* *Je ne fais pas de sport tous les jours.*
*Watch for:* *Je ne fais pas du sport* — the article must change to *de* after negation.

**Item 3:** Choose *aimer, adorer,* or *détester* and write a sentence about a leisure activity you have a strong opinion about. Include the correct article.
*Expected:* *J'adore le cinéma.* / *Je déteste le golf.* / *J'aime beaucoup la randonnée.*
*Watch for:* Partitive instead of definite article.

**Item 4:** Write the *je* form of *préférer.* Use it in a sentence comparing two activities or places.
*Expected:* *préfère.* Sentence: *Je préfère le théâtre au cinéma.* / *Je préfère la montagne à la mer.*
*Watch for:* Missing grave accent (*préfère*).

**Item 5:** Write 3–4 sentences about your leisure life that include: at least one *jouer à* or *faire de*, a frequency adverb, and an opinion using *aimer / adorer / préférer.*
*Expected:* Connected sentences that demonstrate natural integration of all three.
*Minimum pass:* All three elements present; contractions correct.

---

## Scoring Decision Table

| Student output | Decision |
|---|---|
| All 5 oral prompts pass + 4–5 written correct | Ready to move on to Week 11 |
| 4 oral pass + 3–4 written correct | Ready to move on — monitor *préférer* accent in Week 11 speaking |
| *Jouer à / faire de* contractions correct but negation weak | Move to Week 11 — give one negation drill at Session 21 open |
| *Préférer* form inconsistent | Move to Week 11 — assign *préférer* as conjugation review homework |
| Definite article after opinion verbs still wrong | Move to Week 11 — flag for correction in Session 21 warm-up |
| Fewer than 3 oral prompts pass OR fewer than 3 written correct | Flag for instructor follow-up; do not delay Week 11 for whole cohort |

---

## Ready to Move On

Before beginning Week 11 (*avoir mal à*, body parts, pharmacy), a student should be able to:
- [ ] Produce *jouer au/aux/à la* correctly for 3+ sports
- [ ] Produce *faire du/de la/de l'* correctly for 3+ activities
- [ ] Apply the negation rule for *faire de* (→ *pas de*)
- [ ] Use at least 3 frequency adverbs fluently
- [ ] Conjugate *préférer* correctly in *je* form (*préfère*)
- [ ] Express a preference comparing two things (*je préfère A à/au B*)
- [ ] Use *adorer* and *détester* with definite article

---

## Not a Gate

The following items are NOT required for progression to Week 11:
- *Autant que* comparison (extension material)
- Full *préférer* table mastered in all 6 forms (only *je* and *ils* required at this stage)
- Extended cultural activities vocabulary beyond 5–6 items
- *Jouer de + instrument* distinction (not taught in Week 10)
- The difference between *quelquefois* and *parfois* (treat as synonyms)
- Written output beyond 4–6 coherent sentences on the topic
